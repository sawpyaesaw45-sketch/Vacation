Haipa Waterfall

Haipa Waterfall is located in the village of Haipa, in Mong Hsu Township, southern Shan State, Myanmar. The waterfall is opened in the month of Tazaungmone (between November and December) and closed in the month of Kason (May). The best time to visit Haipa Waterfall is during the months of November and December. The waterfall is open during these months, and it is a popular time to visit due to the pleasant weather in Myanmar.
