Ngwe-Saung Beach

Ngwe Saung Beach, also known as Silver Beach, is a popular beach resort located on the western coast of Myanmar, It's situated about 48 km west of Patheinin TownShip in the Ayeyarwady Region and 188 km from Yangon. A popular resort with tourists from October to April.
