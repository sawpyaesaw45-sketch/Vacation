Chaungtha Beach

Chaung Thar is situated in Shwethaungyan Subtownship, Pathein Township, Ayeyarwady Region. It's about a 5-hour drive from Yangon, making it a convenient escape for city dwellers. Travel options include buses, which take approximately 8 hours and 10 minutes. A popular resort with tourists from October to April.
