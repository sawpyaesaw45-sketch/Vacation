Law Pi Ta Waterfall

Law Pi Ta Waterfalls are a scenic natural attraction located near Loikaw in Kayah State, Myanmar. The Lawpita Waterfalls are approximately 45.15 miles from Loikaw. The Law Pi Ta waterfall has a height of 280 feet. The best time to visit Lawpita waterfalls is during the dry season, which typically runs from October to May.
