Indawgyi Lake

Indawgyi Lake is located in Mohnyin Township, Kachin State, in the northern part of Myanmar. It is known as Myanmar's largest lake and the third-largest in Southeast Asia. The lake is 546 feet (166 m) above sea level and is the main feature of the Indawgyi Lake Wildlife Sanctuary which is recognized as a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.It has a length of 14 miles (23 km), width of seven miles and an area of 100 square miles (260 km2). The best time to visit Indawgyi Lake is during the dry season, from October to May.
